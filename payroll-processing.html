Comprehensive Payroll Processing

We ensure that employees are paid accurately, on time. Even if you employ one employee, you now have a payroll process to run a successful business. The payroll process is important for any business and is no longer just a matter of paying an employee's salary into a bank account at the end of each period. Businesses now have a host of functions associated with the paying of employees, ensuring compliance with:

  • **Department of Labour for UIF** – monthly electronic submission regarding staff
  • **Department of Labour for COIDA** (Compensation of Injuries & Diseases Act) – Annual electronic Return
  • **Employment Equity** – storing of information for this application
  • **Garnishments** – payments and maintenance of the balances
  • **Leave** – keeping accurate leave records
  • **SARS**
    • Monthly payment of PAYE, SDL, & UIF.
    • Mid-year & Tax Year End submissions and recons.
    • Issuing of Tax Certificates (IRP5's).
  • **STATS SA (Statistics South Africa)** – should a company get selected to complete this return, S1A/S3A are sent quarterly returns to be completed with details such as headcounts, salaries, overtime, bonus, etc.
  • **3rd party payments & recons** e.g. Medical Aid, Retirement Funds (Pension or Provident Funds), Discovery Life
