Services

Comprehensive Payroll & Tax Compliance Services

Our goal is to make payroll and compliance a seamless part of your business. We handle the complexities so you can focus on what matters most.

Payroll Processing

Accurate & Timely Payments

We handle all aspects of your payroll run, from calculating salaries and deductions to generating payments and secure payslips for each employee. We ensure your staff is paid on time, every time, with 100% accuracy and confidentiality.

Tax Compliance

SARS Submissions & IRP5s

We manage all your tax-related payroll obligations. Our services include preparing and submitting your EMP201 monthly returns, balancing your bi-annual EMP501 submissions, and generating and filing IRP5s for your employees at tax year-end.

Statutory Returns

UIF, COIDA & EE Submissions

Staying compliant with South African labour laws can be complex. We simplify this process by managing your UIF declarations, submitting your annual COIDA returns for a Letter of Good Standing, and completing your Employment Equity (EE) returns on your behalf.

Customised Reporting

Insights & Analytics

Go beyond simple payslips with our custom reporting. We provide detailed payroll reports that offer valuable insights into your company's payroll expenses, leave balances, and other key metrics, all tailored to your specific needs.
