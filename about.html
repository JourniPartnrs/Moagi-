Our Story

We Handle the Numbers So You Can Focus on Your Business


Since 2013, Moagi Payroll Services has been helping businesses save time and improve employee satisfaction by providing an accurate, reliable, and stress-free payroll experience.


Our founder, Moagi Sebothoma, spent over a decade in the payroll industry before starting her own company. She realized that for a business to thrive, its employees need to feel valued and secure. A happy, committed workforce is essential for productivity. This passion for simplifying a critical task is what drives our team today.


We are dedicated to providing accurate and timely payroll services, tax compliance, and customized reporting. Using the secure, online SAGE payroll system, we can serve clients from Johannesburg to anywhere in the country. We handle the numbers so you can focus on growing your business.


Our Mission


To empower businesses by simplifying payroll and ensuring flawless compliance, allowing owners to reclaim their time and focus on what they do best.

Our Vision


To be the most trusted and reliable payroll partner for small to medium-sized businesses across South Africa, known for our precision, integrity, and exceptional service.

Our Values


Integrity: We are committed to honesty and transparency in all our dealings.


Accuracy: We pride ourselves on meticulous attention to detail to ensure error-free payroll.


Partnership: We work with our clients, providing dedicated support as a true extension of their team.
