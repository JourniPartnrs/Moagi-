Leave

Leave = Money, be it annual, sick or any type of leave.

Moagi Payroll Services will manage your leave, by maintaining your records, calculating leave taken and accruing monthly entitlements.

Provision: employees get 15 days per annum in order to accrue 125 days per month. The leave provision for the month is R125 multiplied by rate per day (1/25 X Rate). According to BCEA leave is calculated on basic salary only permanent, allowance, Overtime & Commission over 13 week period and company contributions to Medical Aid or Retirement Fund.

Leave Liability: This is the actual value of the leave for an employee. For example, if an employee has 10 days' vacation – then the leave liability is the value of the daily rate of pay multiplied by the 10 days due.

Failure to maintain leave records could cost employer a lot of money

Sick Leave: a five-day worker is entitled to 30 days over a three-year cycle and a six-day worker is entitled to 36 days over a three-year period.

The following leave types do not alter the balances but a control need to be kept.

Family Responsibility: this leave can only be taken in the following circumstances: death of spouse, child, grandparents, adoptive parents, parent, siblings – ill health of a child that is fully dependant on the employee.

Study – has to be in the field of work.

Unpaid – this will alter the balances but a control needs to be kept on the total leave balance.
