Leave Management

Leave = Money, be it annual, sick or any type of leave.

Moagi Payroll Services will manage your leave, by maintaining all your records, capturing all leave taken and accruing monthly entitlements.

Provisions:

employees get 15 days per annum in order to accrue 1,25 days per month. The leave provision for the month is 1.25 multiplied by rate per day (1.25 X RPD = ) -According to BCEA leave is calculated on basic salary, any permanent allowance, Overtime & Commission over 13 week period and company contributions to Medical Aid or Retirement Fund).


Leave Liability:

This is the actual value of the leave for an employee. For example, if an employee has 10 days’ vocation – then the leave liability is the value of the daily rate of pay multiplied by the 10 days due.

Failure to maintain leave records could cost employer a lot of money

Sick Leave:

a five-day worker is entitled to 30 days over a three-year cycle and a six-day worker is entitled to 36 days over a three-year period.

The following leave types do not alter the balances but a control need to be kept.


Family Responsibility –

This leave can only be taken in the following circumstances: death of spouse, child, grandparents, adoptive parents, parent, siblings- ill health of a child that is fully dependant on the employee


The following leave types do not alter the balances but a control need to be kept.

  • Study-has to be in the field of work

  • Unpaid-this will impact the salary and update the total leave balance.

  • Maternity Leave and Parental Leave
