SARS Tax Submissions

Tax season doesn't have to be a source of stress. We take the hassle out of SARS submissions by managing your company's monthly PAYE, SDL, and UIF payments, as well as the crucial tax year-end submissions.

Staying compliant with SARS tax submissions can be challenging. Moagi Payroll Services takes the stress out of mid-year and year-end submissions, ensuring that all employee tax data aligns with payments and that every employee receives the correct tax certificate.

Book Balancing

We reconcile your August and February submissions against payments already made, ensuring every number is accurate.

Full Compliance

We ensure your business is fully compliant with all SARS requirements, including the correct registration of new employees.

IRP5s Issuing

We handle the annual issuing of IRP5s to all your employees, removing this significant administrative burden from your plate.

What this service includes

  • Monthly PAYE, SDL, and UIF submissions via e-filing.
  • Mid-year (August) and year-end (February) reconciliations.
  • Preparation and submission of EMP201 & EMP501 returns
  • Issuing IRP5 and IT3(a) certificates to employees.
  • Balancing payments made to SARS with actual payroll data.
  • Registering new employees for tax numbers where required.
Benefits

For your company

  • Complete compliance with SARS regulations.
  • Avoidance of fines and penalties for errors or late submissions.
  • Smooth audits with accurate reconciliations.
Benefits

For your employees

  • Correct and timely IRP5 certificates for personal tax filing.
  • Confidence that their tax records are up to date.
  • Hassle-free SARS interactions for new employees.
